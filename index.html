DJ Maxim Dokalenko

Mixing vinyls with booze since ~2kO2. For the first 10 years, it was mostly Drum & Bass with an essence of Rap. Afterwards, the BPM grew into the breakcore and speedcore range. Now trying to erase the borders between genres, but also playing single-style mixes as well. Played mostly across Slovakia in clubs and at open-air rave parties, but had some gigs in Czechia, Austria, and Russia as well. Maxim Dokalenko is a Lazy Bastard. Check the gallery for pieces from the early phases of his history, known as DJ Arrow.
