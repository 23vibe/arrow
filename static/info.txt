Maxim Dokalenko - formerly known as DJ Arrow - started with playing around 2002, heavily influenced by Drum&Bass parties in Bratislava clubs DUNA and U:Club/Účko/Subclub by Signall and Drumatique collectives.
A wise man once said - it is not the art to play good, the art is to play good under any conditions! About 2012 DJ Arrow updated his nickname with the best fitting characteristics - Dokalenko.
Dj Arrow and the Broken Business Crew organized regular Drum & Bass Thursdays in DUNA musiclab and occasional weekend parties in Subclub, Bratislava.
